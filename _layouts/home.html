How we work

product-based approach

We develop components as we’re working with applications. This helps us to prioritize what to build and immediately test what we’re building.

embedded ambassadors

Clarity ambassadors work directly with product teams, providing design and development support. Ambassadors can help convert existing code to Clarity and influence Clarity with elements from your product.

principles

Our iterative work cycles embrace design, research, implementation, and review. We learn from products and customer segments and feed this knowledge back into Clarity, applying it to products.
