Highlights

  • Expose a close event in the alert angular component

    Allows developers to bind callbacks to the alert close event in their templates as in the following example:

    
    <clr-alert [clrAlertType]="'alert-success'" (clrAlertClosedChange)="yourMethod()">
         …...…
    </clr-alert>
