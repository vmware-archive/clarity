Highlights

  • Improved accessibility and internationalization support for Clarity Icons

    With 0.10.12, users can add a custom title to their icons through the title attribute, which improves the accessibility of the icon by making it perceptible to screen readers. For example: 
    
    <clr-icon shape="home" title="homepage icon"></clr-icon>
    In the example above, when the icon gets a focus, screen readers will catch the title attribute value and say "homepage icon" (#1408)
