Highlights

  • New alerts pager for multiple app level alerts

    With 0.10.13, you can now have multiple app level alerts that with a simple pagination arrow to navigate through them. Just wrap app level alerts in the clr-alerts component like this example. (#1530) 
    
    <clr-alerts>
        <clr-alert [clrAlertType]="'info'" [clrAlertAppLevel]="true">...</clr-alert>
        <clr-alert [clrAlertType]="'warning'" [clrAlertAppLevel]="true">...</clr-alert>
        <clr-alert [clrAlertType]="'danger'" [clrAlertAppLevel]="true">...</clr-alert>
    </clr-alerts>
