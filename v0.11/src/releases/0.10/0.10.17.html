Highlights

  • Introducing Compact Datagrid

    With Compact Datagrid, you can increase the information density of your Datagrid or decrease the amount of space it takes up on a screen. To make your Datagrid compact, just add the datagrid-compact class to it.

  • More icons for Clarity Icons

    With 0.10.17, Clarity has introduced new icons such as accessibility-1, accessibility-2, bitcoin, flask, hard-drive, etc. Please refer to the Clarity Icons page for the all available icons.
