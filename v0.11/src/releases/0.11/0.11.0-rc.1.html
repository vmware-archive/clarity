  • Deprecated Entities

    As of v0.11.0-rc.1 we have deprecate many older clarity entities names (components, directives, services) to move to align the Clarity codebase with standardize naming convention across components. Both names are available during the 0.11.x release cycle, allowing you to migrate over the release before they are removed in 0.12.x.
